Billing: Star Dropship Wallet

Each Wallet can only be used to pay for orders in the same currency (e.g. you can't pay for an order in GBP from your EUR Wallet). It's not possible to transfer money from one wallet to another. More on Star Dropship Wallets in our FAQ.

Benefits of using a Star Dropship Wallet

Secure payments and deceased fall rate. Adding moneny to your is safe and easy. Plus, by having funds in your Wallet, you'll avoid the risk of failed orders due to insufficent funds.
Fewer transactions, lower costs! Depending on your payment method and bank, yo may get charged a transaction fee each time you get charged an order. By keeping money in your Star Dropship Wallet, you'll save up on those extra transaction fees.
Fewer pending payments on your credit card. Too many small charges and pending payments can be seen as fraudulent activity by some credit card providers which causes your card to be blocked.
